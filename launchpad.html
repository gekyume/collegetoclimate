A 4 week program dedicated to educating college students on the climate space, and a how-to guide on how to land your first job. Come listen to young professionals speak on their climate journeys, sharpen your outreach skills, and build your identity capital. Apply to join our Flagship Launchpad program for this Summer!

Who is this Course For?

We developed this course for undergraduate and graduate college students, looking to find their footing in the climate space. Whether you are a finance/ environmental science/public policy major, currently working or are in school, we want to give you a full understanding of the climate space, and how to find your first job.

What is the Course Structure?

Our Launchpad program is a four week, ~3 hour/week commitment that combines a speaker series with workshops and soft skill challenges. You can find a full program outline below.

The are plenty of opportunities to increase your engagement with the course. We offer a Portfolio Project Development Track to give you the opportunity to work with fellow cohort students to develop a blog, newsletter, app, and more to spruce up your resume.

What Will Get Out of This Course?

You will:

  • Get to network with young professionals
  • Learn how to use climate job boards, such as Climatebase
  • Gain a full understanding of one of the major sectors in climate tech
  • Tailor your resume for climate work
  • Get exclusive access to microinternships with climate startups (upon completion of the course)
  • Join a community of like-minded students
